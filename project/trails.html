Edinburgh Trails

Queen’s Drive around Holyrood Park

Starting off with a very short and easy trail. Only taking about 1.5-2 hours total, this trail is short and it’s flat terrain makes it easy for those who are new to hiking or aren’t very athletic. Take caution though as cars do drive through this area.

Edinburgh Castle to Holyrood Palace

Another short and easy trail perfect for a stroll. This trail takes place along the Royal Mile and only takes .5-1 hour to complete. Perfect for those who don’t want to commit to long hikes and perfect for discovering the hustle and bustle of Edinburgh!

Arthur’s Seat and Saint Anthony’s Chapel

Moving onto a more moderate trail, this trail takes about 1.5-2 hours to complete. It is a hill walk in the center of Edinburgh. If you enjoy more elevated trails and a nice scenic route, this one is perfect for you.

Water of Leith Walkway

This trail is harder than the previously mentioned trails. This one takes over 5 hours to complete and the terrain is a combination of paved pathways and rough paths. Near the path, there are plenty of places to explore. If you’re looking for a place to explore and take your time with, this trail is a must!
