Historical Sights in Edinburgh

Edinburgh Castle

Of course we have to start off with the most famous sight in Edinburgh, the Edinburgh Castle! Come visit the oldest fortified building in Edinburgh. There are plenty of tours provided so you can explore all of Edinburgh Castle!

Mary King's Close

Discover the underground close and the only preserved 17th century street! Get a glimpse into the past of Edinburgh from the Medieval days to the plague.

Dean Village

If you want to get away from the hustle and bustle of the main streets, this little village is perfect for you. Filled with historic buildings and scenic riverside views, this place is perfect for a stroll.
