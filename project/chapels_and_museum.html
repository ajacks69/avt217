Chapels and Museums

St. Giles Cathedral

Founded in 1124, this church has been a working church for over 900 years. The cathedral offers short guided walking tours where they talk about the history of the cathedral. The cathedral is open to the public as well so you can wander around yourself.

National Museum of Scotland

This museum has pretty much everything. From science and art to Scottish history to world culture. Whatever you want to learn about, they have it. It is also free admission so it’s a great place to visit on a budget!

National War Museum

Located in Edinburgh Castle, this museum is a great way to learn about Scotland’s Military history. From letters sent by soldiers to actual artifacts used in war, this museum is a perfect way to broaden your knowledge.
