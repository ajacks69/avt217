Hotels and Inns

Need a place to stay during your visit? We got you covered! We’ve compiled the top four inns and hotels for your stay!

The Balmoral Hotel

Prefer a more luxurious stay? Enjoy your time at the Balmoral Hotel! Where Old town meets New! This hotel has elegant rooms and views for all to enjoy! Spas, restaurants, and even bars are all in this hotel for your enjoyment! A bit on the pricier side, but if you don’t mind that, then this is perfect for you.

Cheval- The Edinburgh Grand

Another luxurious pick, this hotel is located right in the center of Edinburgh. You’ll be just steps away from Edinburgh castle and the Royal Mile. Plenty of excursions nearby for you and your loved ones to enjoy. If you want spacious rooms amd various activities, this is the perfect fit for you.

Motel One Edinburgh Royal

Tight on a budget? This motel is perfect! The rooms may be a bit on the smaller side but they are pristine. The historic Grassmarket is less than half a mile away and has a variety of bars and restaurants at your fingertips. Cockburn Street and the Royal mile are a simple five minute walk from this motel!

Six Brunton Place

Want a place with a little more charm? This cozy guest house is the perfect home away from home. With delicious breakfast and quaint rooms, this place has the perfect Scottish charm for everyone!
